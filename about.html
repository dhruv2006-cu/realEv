About Event-Mate

Connecting the university community through events

Our Mission

Event-Mate was created to streamline event management across the university, making it easier for students, faculty, and staff to discover, create, and participate in campus events. Our mission is to foster community engagement and enhance the university experience through a centralized event platform.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a group of university students frustrated with the disjointed event management systems across campus departments, Event-Mate began as a class project in the Computer Science department. After winning the university's innovation award, the project was adopted campus-wide and has since grown to serve over 950 users annually.
