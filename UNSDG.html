8

Decent Work and Economic Growth

Promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all.

Key Targets:

  • Sustain per capita economic growth
  • Achieve higher levels of economic productivity
  • Promote youth employment and education
  • Protect labor rights and promote safe working environments
Global progress: 65% towards 2030 targets
9

Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation.

Key Targets:

  • Develop quality, reliable infrastructure
  • Promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization
  • Increase access to financial services and markets
  • Enhance scientific research and technological capabilities
Global progress: 55% towards 2030 targets
17

Partnerships for the Goals

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.

Key Targets:

  • Strengthen domestic resource mobilization
  • Enhance global macroeconomic stability
  • Promote technology development and transfer
  • Encourage effective public-private partnerships
Global progress: 45% towards 2030 targets

Goal Connections

Resources

The three goals are deeply interconnected. SDG 8 (Economic Growth) depends on SDG 9 (Innovation and Infrastructure) to create sustainable jobs and industries. Both require SDG 17 (Partnerships) to mobilize resources and share knowledge globally.

How You Can Contribute:

  • As an individual: Support ethical businesses, develop job skills, engage in local economic initiatives
  • As a business: Invest in sustainable infrastructure, create decent jobs, collaborate with partners
  • As a policymaker: Create enabling environments for innovation and fair economic growth
