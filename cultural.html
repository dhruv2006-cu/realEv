Rangmanch 2025 - Chitkara University Cultural Fest

Chitkara University recently lit up with vibrant colors and unbounded energy as it hosted its much-awaited annual cultural extravaganza, Rangmanch 2025. The fest served as a grand platform for students from various disciplines to come together and celebrate the rich tapestry of art, music, dance, and cultural heritage that defines the spirit of youth.

From soulful musical performances and electrifying dance battles to captivating theatrical plays and creative arts displays, Rangmanch 2025 was a true celebration of talent and tradition. The fest attracted not only Chitkara’s own students but also enthusiastic participants from colleges across the region, creating a cultural melting pot like no other.

Highlights of the event included the much-anticipated fashion show, the street play competition that brought social issues to light, and a fusion night that mesmerized the audience with a blend of classical and contemporary performances. Food stalls, handmade art exhibitions, and cultural booths added even more flavor to the festivities.

The atmosphere throughout the event was electric — filled with laughter, applause, and heartfelt cheers. Rangmanch 2025 beautifully reflected Chitkara University's dedication to nurturing not just academic excellence but also artistic expression and cultural appreciation.

As the curtains closed on this memorable celebration, students left with lasting memories, new friendships, and a deepened sense of belonging. Rangmanch 2025 wasn't just a fest — it was an experience that brought hearts together through the rhythm of culture and celebration.
